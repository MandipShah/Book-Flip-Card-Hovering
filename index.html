The Jungle Book

The Jungle Book is a collection of stories written by Rudyard Kipling. The stories are set in a forest in India and feature a young boy named Mowgli, who is raised by wolves. The book is known for its vivid descriptions of the jungle and its inhabitants, including Shere Khan the tiger and Baloo the bear.
